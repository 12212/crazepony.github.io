Binding & Flying Procedure

a.Turn on transmitter, create new mode setup(modified original setup created before also available ), the Mode must be D8, turn off after setup.

b. Power on quadcopter, red LED flashing rapodly on receiver after 6 seconds, the receiver is ready for binding. Then turn on the transmitter, enter the binding mode, the Red LED will be solid, means binded successfully with default mode SBUS.

Turn on the transmitter between 6 to 30 second to binded with SBUS mode; Turn on the transmitter after 30 seconds to binded with PPM mode. Flight controller default SBUS mode.

c. Each flight, turn on the transmitter firstly, then power on the quadcopter, the receiver Red LED solid, means binded, with RSSI and voltage return.

d. Switch SA key(middle position ), the Blue LED light on the receiver, means quadcopter unlocked and ready to fly.

Crazepony 90mm BNF was set SA(CH5) to ARM/DISARM the motor before shipping, you can also customize it

Q:Can i enjoy it if i am a drone hobby beginner?

Its not a beginners quad.You will need a descent transmitter.

You will need to know how to program cleanflight/betaflight.

Most important,you should have 5.8G goggles to enjoy the FPV experience.

Q:can we buy the receiver separately?or replace it.

The receiver is connected to the fc board with 3 wires (PPM/5V/GND).Unsolder them and solder in your preferred micro receiver.For example,receiver can be replaced with a Spektrum DSMX Satt.

Q:How to bind the FrSky X9D transmitter?

Bind process(S-BUS output): shut down your FrSky X9D transmitter,power on receiver.It goes into binding state 6 seconds later with LED flashing.Then power on the transmitter and start its bind function.When the LED is solid on,binding success.

Bind process(CPPM output): shut down your FrSky X9D transmitter,power on receiver.It goes into binding state 6 seconds later with LED flashing.Wait for 30 seconds.Then power on the transmitter and start its bind function.When the LED is flashing every second,binding success.

You need to bind the receiver with your transmitter the first time.When using later,you MUST power on you transmitter before power on the receiver.

Q:I want more information about the brush flight controller.

The FC is designed by Makerfire team.It is based on SPRacing F3_EVO_Brush FC,full Cleanflight/Betaflight compatibility on a STM32 F303 style processor.The size is 26mm x 26mm, fully compatible with the Blade Inductrix quad frame and mounting pattern.

Cleanflight GUI Set Up

CLI页面：在文本框中输入

set motor_pwm_rate=1000
save

Ports页面：开启UART2的Serial RX，然后点击右下角的Save and Reboot按钮。

Configuration页面：ESC/Motor Features，开启MOTOR_STOP

Receiver Mode，选择RX_SERIAL；Serial Receiver Provider，选择SBUS。然后点击右下角的Save and Reboot按钮。

Modes页面： ARM，点击Add Range；ANGLE，点击Add Range。然后点击右下角的Save按钮。

LED Flight Indicator

Indicator Normal
Red LED solid(FC) Power on
Blue LED flashing(FC) NOT armed yet
Blue LED solid(FC) Motor armed
Red LED flashing(Rx) Binding status
Red LED solid(Rx) Connected with Tx successfully with SBUS output
Red LED solid(Rx) and flash 10 seconds Connected with Tx successfully with PPM output
