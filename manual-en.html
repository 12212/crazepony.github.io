RC Transmitter User Guide

1.Place the Crazepony on a stable surface,like the floor.The Arraw on the center of the PCB indicate the front.

2.Set the Crazepony RC switch to ON side ,then quadcopter.About 9s later,blue LEDs on M1/M2 flashing,sensor are calibrated.

3.Press the "-" button on RC to start IMU calibration.Press the "+" button on RC to start the motors with speed.

4.The RC tansmitter is throttle on the left joystick.Head free mode.Press the "+" button again to stop motors.

Android Client User Guide

There is now an offical version in the Google Play Store,Scan the QR right to install it.

Click the Connect button to scan and connect the Crazepony quadcopter.For the first time,the Bluetooth pair key word is 1234.

Click the Unarm button to start the motors and enjoy to fly.

Headfree button for head free mode,Altihold button for altitude hold mode and IMUCali button for IMU calibrate.

LED Flight Indicator

Crazepony has 4 blue LEDs mounted on each arms M1/M2/M3/M4.The LEDs indicate the status and operation.

Indicator Warning Solution
M1/M2/M3/M4 Flash Rorating Remote Control Signal Lost Power the RC or Android APP
M1/M2 and M3/M4 Flash Alternatively IMU Error Press the "-" Button on RC
M1/M2/M3/M4 Flashing Low Battery Warning Connect the USB to Charge
Indicator Normal
M1/M2 Flashing Ready to Fly
M1/M2 Solid Blue IMU Calibrating
M1 Flashing Config the BT Module

Tips:

Set the switch to CHG side for charge.The red LED blind down means charge is over.
